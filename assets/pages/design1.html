Graphic Design Work:

May 2025

Pacific Garbage Patch Poster

This graphic was for a design class I was taking where we had to make a poster focusing on a major historical event. Initially, my topic was Watergate/Nixon, but I found this topic dull, so I switched to the Great Pacific Garbage Patch. Finally, after a couple of iterations, I ended up with this.

April 2024

UFO Movie Poster

For a class focused on design, I created this graphic. For this one, I had to make a poster utilizing a custom font that my class and I produced earlier in the semester. Moreover, I could only use two colors: yellow and light blue. Here, I used varying opacities to get the green you see.

Typography Post Design
